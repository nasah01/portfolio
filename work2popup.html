Projects and Work (During Graduation)

Portfolio on GitHub (BSc - 2nd Year)

Summary of the Project:

As per the requirement and instructions a portfolio (akin website) was developed to be posted on GitHub. The content of the portfolio consisted of the following -- About Myself, Education, Projects and Works and Achievements. In addition, Contact details, Personal Skills, Technical Skills and Interests were also displayed.


Role:

Designer. Developer, Tester and Evaluator. (Individual Capacity)

The portfolio was developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The GitHub is case sensitive, hence the names of files and images were named in lowercase.

There is an index.html page, where content related to About Myself, Education, and Achievements are placed under different sections and under relevant headings.

A dedicated page for 'Projects and Work (During Graduation)' has also been developed. Summary information about each work and project has been displayed, along with link to view details of the corresponding work and project. Clicking on this link, the details are displayed in a popup screen.

Two different photos were uploaded. The primary photo was displayed, on rolling over the primary photo, the secondary photo could be viewed.
